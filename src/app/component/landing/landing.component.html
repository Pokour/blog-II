Why are we here?

We are an educational platform in a mission to create an ecosystem which breeds young minds and help them pursue their hobby in electronics & robotics. There is a lot of talk around robotics and the web has become a dumping ground of such stories. We are here to solve that exact problem. We aim to clear the clutter and provide a platform for students to guide them through their road to learning.

More About Us

What do we provide?

Library

It is called a library, because it is one! Apart from the usual click bait articles, you will find every other article as a valuable source to strengthen your knowledge in the subject.

Projects

This is where the fun part comes. Apply the lesson learnt! We welcome all the nerdy heads out there to collaborate with us and be a part of exciting projects which will help you pursue a career in modern technology.

Products

We have built a marketplace for all that you need to make believe the idea in your head. Checkout the shop section in the website and find your item in need. Also, in the name of good hope, we will start manufacturing the products which we believe might take off in the market.
